Benefits of UFV Stride+

  • Improved Health: Encourages daily movement and builds healthy habits.
  • Mental Wellness: Reduces stress and improves mood through exercise.
  • Academic Performance: Active students demonstrate better focus and discipline.
  • Community Connection: Students engage in challenges and group runs.
  • UFV Image: Positions UFV as a leader in student wellness technology.
